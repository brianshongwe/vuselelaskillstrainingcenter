Vuselela Skills Training School is a non-profit vocational training institution based in Benoni, Gauteng. The organisation intends to establish a vocational skills training centre in Daveyton, Ekurhuleni to address the growing challenge of youth unemployment and lack of technical skills among young people.

The centre will focus on equipping young people with practical artisan and vocational skills that will enable them to become employable, self-reliant, and capable of contributing meaningfully to the South African economy.

Youth unemployment in the City of Ekurhuleni remains extremely high. Many young people lack access to practical training opportunities that can prepare them for employment in skilled trades. Vuselela Skills Training School aims to bridge this gap by providing high-quality vocational training programmes delivered by experienced and retired professional artisans.

The organisation therefore seeks support and partnership with the Department of Higher Education and Training and other stakeholders to establish and operate a fully functional skills training centre.

Popular Courses

Electrical Engineering

Electrical Engineering

Electrical engineering is a dynamic field focusing on the study, design, and application of equipment, devices, and systems that utilize electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism.

Plumbing

Plumbing

Plumbing is any system that conveys fluids for a wide range of applications. Plumbing uses pipes, valves, plumbing fixtures, tanks, and other apparatuses to convey fluids.

Carpentry

Carpentry

Carpentry is a skilled trade focused on cutting, shaping, and installing building materials—primarily wood—for structural frameworks,, staircases, doors, and furniture. It requires high precision in measuring and using both hand and power tools, acting as a crucial component of construction and renovations.

Welding

Welding

Welding is a fabrication process that joins materials, typically metals or thermoplastics, by using high heat to melt the parts together, often with a filler material, allowing them to cool into a single, solid piece. It is essential for construction, manufacturing, and repair, often using techniques like MIG, TIG, or Stick welding.

Building and construction

Building and construction

Building construction is the process of adding structures to real property, transforming designs into physical reality through planning, site preparation, foundation work, framing, and finishing. It involves coordinating labor, materials, and equipment to create functional, safe, and sustainable buildings such as homes, offices, and industrial facilities.

Mechanical Trades

Mechanical Trades

Mechanical trades are highly skilled professions focused on the installation, repair, and maintenance of complex machinery, infrastructure, and environmental systems, such as HVAC, plumbing, and pipefitting. These trades are essential for building infrastructure, utilizing technical skills to manage systems that provide comfort and safety.
